Privacy Policy

DiskLite · Effective date: July 15, 2026

DiskLite does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything the app analyzes stays on your Mac.

Overview

DiskLite is a local disk-analysis utility for macOS. It runs entirely on your device inside the Apple sandbox. We — the developer of DiskLite — do not operate any server that receives your information, and the app is designed to work without an account, a login, or an internet connection.

Information We Collect

None. DiskLite does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation, sign-in, or user profile is required.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or usage-telemetry SDKs are included.
  • No file contents, file names, paths, or scan results ever leave your device.
  • No cookies, device identifiers, location, or contact data are gathered.

How the App Works

When you point DiskLite at a folder or disk, it reads file and folder sizes locally to produce the on-screen visualizations and reports. This information is processed in memory and stored only on your own Mac. It is never uploaded anywhere.

If you use the cleanup or uninstall features, DiskLite only moves the items you select to the macOS Trash on your own Mac, after your explicit confirmation. Nothing is deleted outright, and nothing is ever sent off your device.

Network Access

DiskLite does not require internet access to function and does not send your data over the network.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing to sell, rent, or share. DiskLite does not integrate any third-party data-collection services.

Data Retention & Deletion

Any data DiskLite creates (such as cached scan results or settings) is stored locally on your device and is removed when you delete that data within the app or uninstall DiskLite.

Children's Privacy

DiskLite does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
