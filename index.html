Not another single-purpose tracker. Digital Life brings time, files, energy and storage together — beautifully, and entirely on your device.

App usage & screen time

See which apps you actually use and for how long, with daily rankings and app icons — plus screen time vs. the previous period.

File activity

Created, deleted and modified files — grouped by directory, type and the app that made the change.

Energy & battery

Battery and power trends, plus a per-app energy ranking that shows what's really draining your Mac.

Disk space & cleanup

Scan your disk to find the biggest folders and files, then clear out junk to reclaim space.

Daily / weekly / monthly reports

Clear, polished reports that turn raw activity into insight — with network traffic and CPU/memory top lists.

Full control of your data

Clear data by day or category, and export everything to JSON or CSV. It's your data — always.

Privacy by design

Your life stays on your Mac.

This is what Digital Life cares about most. There is no server to send your data to — because we never built one.

  • 100% local. Everything is written to a database on your own Mac, with no network reporting whatsoever.
  • One optional network call. Only when you click "Check for Updates" — it fetches a version number and sends none of your data.
  • Optional encryption. Turn on database encryption in Settings — the key lives in your system Keychain.
